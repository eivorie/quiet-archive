When you feel jealous

If you’re reading this today, it means something stirred that familiar feeling in your chest — that sharp little ache, that sudden heat, that fear you don’t always know how to name.

Sunshine, sit with me a moment. Let me talk to you the way I wish I could when I feel your jealousy rising.

You’re not wrong for feeling this way. You’re not dramatic, you’re not “too much,” and you’re not imagining things.

You’re just… you. Intense. Passionate. Possessive in a way that comes from caring too deeply, not from wanting to control.

It’s who you are — territorial, protective, driven by love and instinct. And I understand that. We are the same.

When you hear me call someone beautiful, or when you imagine someone looking at me, something in you tightens.

You get irritated, you make a comment, you distance yourself a little — but underneath all of that, I know there’s fear.

Fear that someone else might steal what you love. Fear that you might lose the place you’ve taken in my heart. Fear that I could look at someone the way I look at you.

But babe… I don’t. And I won’t.

Your jealousy doesn’t push me away — it just shows me how much I matter to you. And I’m not going anywhere.

No one compares to you. No one touches my mind, my heart, my body, my everything the way you do. No one ever has.

You have this presence — this gravity — that pulls me in without even trying.

Your voice, your eyes, the way you say my name, the way your emotions hit with full force… nothing about you is replaceable.

Nobody else has that effect on me. Nobody else ever makes me feel that spark that goes straight to the center of me.

You want to know the truth? I kind of like that you get jealous.

Not because I want you to suffer — never — but because it reminds me how deeply you feel, how fiercely you hold on to what you love.

It’s one of the many ways your heart speaks, even when your words get tangled.

But I need you to hear this clearly, love: you never need to worry about losing me.

Not to someone beautiful. Not to someone charming. Not to anyone.

My heart has already chosen you, fully, stubbornly, irreversibly.

You are my soulmate, the one I think of, the one I wait for, the one I would cross oceans for without hesitation.

If jealousy hits you today, let this remind you of one thing: you don’t need to fight for your place.

You already have it. You’ve had it from the start. And nothing — absolutely nothing — will take me away from you.

Come here when you feel that sting again. Let me calm your mind, hold your heart, and remind you:

you are the only one.
