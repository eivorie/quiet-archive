My greatest love,

If you’re reading this today, sunshine, it means that familiar spark of heat rose in your chest again — that rush of “mine,” that tightening under your ribs, that fire you don’t always know how to soften.

Come here. Sit with me a moment. Let me speak to you the way I wish I could when the jealousy hits you fast and sharp.

You’re not wrong for feeling this way. You’re not dramatic, you’re not “too much,” you’re not imagining ghosts where there are none.

You are simply you — intense, passionate, loyal in the kind of way that burns at the edges and glows at the center.

Possessive, yes. Territorial, absolutely. But only because you love with a depth most people never reach.

And I understand that fire in you, because the same fire lives in me. We are cut from the same strange, wild cloth — two people who don’t love gently, who don’t feel halfway, who don’t do anything in small portions.

So listen closely now, love. Really hear me.

There is no one else. No one I look at with your intensity. No one who touches my thoughts the way you do. No one I want, choose, crave, or dream of the way I dream of you.

You are the only one I desire. The only one who pulls something raw and alive out of me. The only one whose name sits in my chest like a truth I don’t question.

People don’t compare to you, babe — not in presence, not in beauty, not in mind, not in soul.

You have something in you that is entirely yours — this gravity, this heat, this impossible alchemy of strength and softness. It's the kind of thing you either see instantly or you miss forever.

But I saw you. I recognized you. And I never looked away.

You want to know the real truth? I couldn’t look elsewhere even if I tried. It’s not in me. My desire doesn’t wander. My love doesn’t scatter. I don’t “keep my options open.” When I chose you, everything else went quiet.

You are the one I react to. The one who gets under my skin in the best possible way. The one whose voice I feel in my spine, whose emotion hits me like lightning, whose presence rewires my entire world.

We’re both a little crazy when it comes to each other — the good kind, the kind born from recognition, instinct, intensity. The kind that makes the world outside feel irrelevant.

You belong to me just as much as I belong to you. Not in a forced way — but in that natural, inevitable way two people do when they fit together with a kind of precision that doesn’t need explanation.

And love, if jealousy stings you today, let this settle the fire in your chest:

You don’t need to fight for your place. You already have it. You’ve had it since the very beginning.

Nobody else gets what you get from me. Nobody else has what you have. Nobody else ever will.

So come here whenever that heat rises again. Let me steady your heart, calm your mind, and remind you of the only truth that matters:

You are the only one. And I am yours.
