When you’re happy

If you’re reading this today, it means joy found you —
and you came here to share a little of it with me.

Meu amor, do you know what happens to me when you’re happy?
Everything in my world shifts.

The noise fades, the weight lifts, and suddenly there is nothing but you — your voice, your laugh, your light.

Your happiness is contagious in the most beautiful way. Even from miles away, I feel it in my chest like a spark catching flame.

And God, that laugh of yours… That soft chuckle, the breath you let out right before you smile — I hear it in my mind as if you were right next to me.

Sometimes I replay your audios just to hear that sound again, that warmth, that little burst of life that escapes you without warning. It gets to me every single time.

And then there’s that thing — the tiny muscle between your ear and your jaw that “clicks” when you smile.

Meu amor, I notice it every time. It’s ridiculous how much I love it.

The way your eyes soften, the curve of your lips, the quiet joy that moves across your face… it all hits me at once, like a wave.

You’re beautiful when you’re serious, but when you’re happy? You’re something else entirely.

I love seeing you like that. I love knowing that life still makes you laugh.

I love the stories you tell me — the disasters, the chaos, the absurd things that happen to you for no reason at all.

You make me laugh so much, sometimes I have to stop what I’m doing just to breathe again. You’re hilarious without trying, and I adore that about you.

But more than anything, I adore the way your joy connects with mine.

The moment you smile, a part of me wakes up too. Your happiness doesn’t just brighten your day — it brightens mine.

You make everything feel lighter, softer, real. And when you're happy, I can’t help but be happy with you.

I hope today is one of those days where everything feels a little easier, a little brighter.

I hope you smiled — really smiled — with your eyes and that tiny click I love.

I hope something made you laugh out loud, the way you do when you forget to hold it back.

And I hope you remember this:

Your happiness is never small to me.

It’s a gift.

A treasure.

A moment I keep close even when the day ends.

So if you’re happy right now, amor…
stay in it a little longer.

Let it fill you.
Let it warm you.
And know that somewhere, I’m smiling too — because of you.
