When you're angry

If you’re reading this right now, it means something went wrong — something upset you, something pushed too hard, something stung more than it should have. And you ended up here, with me.

Meu amor, I know how you get when you’re angry. You pull back, your words get sharper, you breathe differently, everything in you tenses.

And honestly? I’m often right there with you.

If you’re angry, there’s a good chance I’m angry too — or hurt, or defensive, or just overwhelmed.

We flare up together. We push, we pull, we misunderstand each other, we get stubborn. That’s us.

But here’s the truth beneath all of it: if you’re feeling something, then it matters.

Your emotions are valid — always. Even when they’re messy. Even when they’re loud. Even when they make no sense on the surface.

You don’t have to hide your anger from me. You don’t have to pretend you’re fine, or swallow it, or act like it’s nothing.

You’re allowed to feel whatever you feel — jealousy, irritation, frustration, fear.

I know those feelings hit you fast. I know they burn hot. And it’s okay.

It doesn’t make you difficult. It doesn’t make you wrong. It just makes you human. It makes you you.

And you know us — we argue, we get sharp, we take things personally, and then…

we breathe. We take a step back. We soften. We listen.

We talk again, properly this time, with the kind of honesty that only comes after the fire.

We choose each other again and again — even on the days we clash.

So if you’re angry right now, sunshine, let me say this: I’m not your enemy.

I’m not on the other side of the fight.

Even if we’re both hurting, even if we’re both stubborn, I’m still here with you, not against you.

Take your time. Breathe. Let the storm pass through you.

You don’t have to fix anything right this second. You don’t have to be calm immediately.

Just stay with me — even in silence — and when you’re ready, we’ll talk.

We always do.

And no matter what sparked your anger today, remember this: I love you.

Not only when you’re soft. Not only when you’re calm.

But also when you’re burning, when you’re raw, when you're trying to protect yourself in the only ways you know how.

You don’t have to be perfect for me. You just have to be you.

And even now — especially now — I’m right here, and I love you.
